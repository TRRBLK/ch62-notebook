##Agile Manifest
12 principles and 4 values

bash````VALUES
- Invididuals and interactions over processes and tools.
- Working product over comprehensive documentation.
- Customer collaboration over contract negotiation.
- Responding to change over following a plan.
```

PRINCIPLES
1. Customer satisfaction through early, continuos delivery of valuable software. 

2. Accept changes even in the final stages of production.

3. Creation of functional software often, between a two-week or two-month 
deadline.

4. Work together with the Business responsable and developers daily while 
the project lasts.

5. Motivated individuals. Proper work station and support, and trust them.

6. Face-to-face communication.

7. Functioning software is the main measurable metric.

8. Sustainable software development. Constant, continuous work.

9. Continuous focus on technical capabilities and structural design.

10. Simplicity.

11. Self-organized teams makes architectures, needs and designs as best as 
possible.

12. Theres a span of time to self reflect and improve our aptitude towards 
efficiency.

Becoming AGILE means to identify uncertainty, solve it and adapt to it 
effectively.

#Benefits
- Less risk of delivering a failed product.
- Project management based on the 4 principles.
- More involvement among the interested parts.
